To install the app:

On iPhones:

  1. Open this page with Safari. You *must* use Safari.
  2. Tap the Share button. It looks like a square with an arrow pointing up.
  3. If you do not see the Share button, tap the three-dot ••• menu first.
  4. Scroll down and tap “Add to Home Screen”.
  5. Tap “Add” in the upper right corner.
  6. The NTL App will now appear on your home screen.
  7. Tap it anytime for quick access to Nashville Talking Library.

This short video shows you how to quickly install the app on an iPhone.

On Android Phones:

  1. Open this page with Chrome. You *must* use Chrome.
  2. If prompted, tap "Install" and follow the prompt.
  3. If not prompted, tap the three-dot ••• menu, then tap "Install" or "Add to Home Screen" and follow the prompt.
  4. The NTL App will now appear on your home screen.
  5. Tap it anytime for quick access to Nashville Talking Library.
