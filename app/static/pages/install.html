To install the app:

On Apple Phones:

  • Open this page with Safari
  • Tap the Share button
  • Tap "Add to Home Screen" and follow the prompt

On Android Phones:

  • Open this page with Chrome
  • If prompted, tap "Install"
  • If not prompted, tap the 3 dots •••, then Tap "Install" or "Add to Home Screen" and follow the prompt
