Privacy Policy

This policy applies to the Nashville Talking Library Mobile App and related audio services. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard information when you use our app.

Information We Collect

We collect limited usage data to help us improve our services and understand how our app is used. This includes:

Server Logs & App Analytics

Like most web services, our servers automatically collect information such as IP addresses, browser type, device type, and usage patterns. These logs are stored securely on our own servers and are not shared with third parties.

Live Stream Analytics

When you listen to our live audio stream, usage data (such as IP address, listening duration, and device type) may be collected by our third-party streaming provider, StreamGuys, to help us monitor and improve streaming performance.

Podcast Analytics

When you listen to our on-demand audio content (podcasts), usage data (such as IP address and device type) may be collected by Podtrac, a third-party podcast analytics provider. This helps us understand listener engagement and improve our offerings.

Data Usage

The data collected is used solely for internal analysis and service improvement. We do not sell or share your personal data with advertisers or other third parties. Please note that StreamGuys and Podtrac may process data according to their own privacy policies.

Your Privacy

We do not collect personally identifiable information (PII) unless you explicitly provide it. Any data collected is used in accordance with applicable privacy laws and best practices.

Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact us.
